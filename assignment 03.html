A Pauper and a Prince

Two boys are born

In 1537, in the city of London, a baby boy was born. His name was Tom Canty.

The Canty family of Fish Street

Several years passed. London was 1,500 years old. It had grown into a big, crowded city with narrow streets of wooden houses. Tom's family lived in Fish Street.

Father Andrew

Father Andrew was an old priest. He had worked for the king and his family. He lived with them in the great Palace of Westminster. But when he became old, the king sent Father Andrew away without any money.
